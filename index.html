About Me

My passion for coding first ignited in 2nd grade when I joined a coding club and began using Scratch block coding. This early, hands-on experience in problem solving was later solidified by taking an 'Introduction to Python' class as a freshman in high school, which guided my academic path to The Ohio State University for Computer Science and Engineering.

Currently, I focus on writing practical applications in Java, C++, and Python. Beyond the code, my experience running a landscaping business has taught me invaluable lessons in client communication and accountability, skills I apply to every project.
